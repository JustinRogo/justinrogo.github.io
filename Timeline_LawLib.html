A Century of Legal Knowledge

University of Connecticut School of Law Library (1921–Present)

1921 - 1931

The Early Years: Downtown Hartford

From rented rooms at 94 Allyn Street to space in the Hartford Trust Building and Chapel Street. The college began with no library, relying on the Bar Library.

1940 - 1964

39 Woodland Street: The Jacobus Mansion

A "veritable mansion" compared to previous quarters. An annex was later built to house book stacks and a reading room.
