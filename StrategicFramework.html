Our Strategic Framework

"Creativity supported. Collaboration fueled. Process shaped with intention."

Access

Evolving Resources

Strategic Direction

Provide accessible, relevant resources that support the needs of the law school community and address the changing landscape of legal information and the legal profession.

Our Goals

  • Curate and preserve a collection that supports research, scholarship, and curricular needs.
  • Enhance accessibility and discovery of electronic and print resources.
  • Promote visibility of the law library and technology departments.

Prepare

Student Success

Strategic Direction

Educate the law school community through formal and informal instruction, supporting student success while preparing them for modern legal practice.

Our Goals

  • Lead curricular development in legal technology and research.
  • Develop specialized instruction and leverage innovative ed-tech solutions.
  • Align instructional services and resources to support student success.
  • Integrate information literacy and critical thinking competencies.

Discover

Advancing Scholarship

Strategic Direction

Create a culture of inquiry and critical thinking by supporting, contributing to, and engaging in research and scholarship.

Our Goals

  • Support discovery by making faculty and student scholarship available online.
  • Strengthen support for scholarly research through training and technical resources.
  • Identify new opportunities to engage with the law school community.

Connect

Community & Justice

Strategic Direction

Serve as a welcoming community hub that supports the academic mission of the law school and the legal information needs of Connecticut communities.

Our Goals

  • Assess user experience to continually improve services.
  • Promote access to justice by honoring our commitment to serve the public.
  • Create, promote, and host events that bring the community together.
