Calculation method: percentage increases are compounded year over year. The 2027 merit component is added as a dollar amount after the 2027 percentage increase. The reclassification increase is applied first, before the scheduled July 1 increases.

Scheduled increases:

  • July 1, 2025: 2.5% General Wage Increase (GWI) + 2% Performance-Based Compensation (PBC)
  • July 1, 2026: 2.5% GWI + 2% PBC
  • July 1, 2027: 2.5% GWI + 1.5% PBC + 0.5% merit component
